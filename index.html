About Us

IIT Roorkee Motorsports is the Formula Student team of IIT Roorkee which participates in Formula SAE competitions, the largest Engineering Competition in the world, by designing, developing and fabricating an open wheel Formula style Race Car. Our debut competition was the FSAE –Australasia in 2011 and since then the team has improved considerably to being the 1st Ranked Electric team in India as per the FSAE Electric rankings...(click to read more)

Faculty Advisors

Prof Akshay Dvivedi

Prof Arup Kumar Das

Prof Pramod Agarwal



Prof Akshay Dvivedi has been a constant source of guidance for the mechanical team of IIT Roorkee Motorsports since 2010. Prof Dvivedi has completed his Ph.D. in Advanced Manufacturing Processes from IIT Roorkee in 2009 and has also won the Outstanding teacher award in 2016. His areas of interest include Advanced Manufacturing Processes, Nontraditional Micro-machining, Metal Matrix Composites, Primary and Secondary Processing of MMC and Quality Management. Prof Dwivedi has stood by our side for all these years and for his constant enthusiasm, dedication and support Team IIT Roorkee Motorsports feels honored to have him as our Faculty Advisor.



Prof Arup Kumar Das has been mentoring the mechanical team of IIT Roorkee Motorsports since the start of his tenure as Assistant Professor at IIT Roorkee in 2014. Prof Das has completed his Ph.D. in mechanical engineering from IIT Kharagpur in 2010 and has also won the young engineer award in 2015. His areas of interest include Two-phase flow, Microfluidics, Boiling and condensation, Augmentation of boiling heat transfer coefficient, Bubble and drop dynamics, wettability gradient and electrowetting. Prof Das has stood by our side for over 4 years now and for his constant enthusiasm, advice and determination Team IIT Roorkee Motorsports feels honored to have him as our Faculty Advisor.



Prof Pramod Agarwal has been a constant source of Motivation for the Electrical team of IIT Roorkee Motorsports since the very first year of our existence. Prof Agarwal has completed his Ph.D. in Microprocessor Controlled Current Source Inverter fed Induction motor drive from IIT Roorkee in 1995. Prof Agarwal has won several awards like the Outstanding teacher award in 2008, University Gold medal, The Union Ministry of Energy: Department of Power Medal and many more. His areas of interest include Electrical Machines, Power Electronics, Microprocessors, Microcomputers Controlled Electric Drives, Electrical Machines, Power Electronics, Microprocessors, Microcomputers Controlled Electric Drives. For his infinite wisdom, abundant knowledge and constant support Team IIT Roorkee Motorsports feels honored to have him as our Faculty Advisor.
